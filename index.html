Pareto Summit 2025

The annual MUN simulation hosted by Pareto at IIT Madras, kicked off its first-ever edition with a G20 FMCBG simulation, with delegations from several universities across three states, organised successfully across two days at the IITM campus, with a prize pool of INR 10,000!

Equilibria 2025

The case study competition hosted online in collaboration with the Economics Club of IISER Bhopal, Equilibria attracted sponsored incentives worth over INR 1,00,000, and had a INR 15,000 cash prize pool in addition to it. Hundreds of students from reputed institutes across the country participated in the multi-round event, with a final few getting selected for dazzling awards, including cash prizes, discounted courses, study-abroad deals, and internships!

Pareto is a student-run club dedicated to exploring the best of economics, analytics, and decision-making. We host workshops, seminars, case competitions, and a flagship MUN committee every year. You can find our Instagram and LinkedIn pages here to know more about what we've been up to.
