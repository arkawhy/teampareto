Equilibria
An online nation-wide case study competition centered on economics, business, finance, analytics, and mathematics, Equilibria saw hundreds of academics from reputed institutions across the country participate in an event co-hosted by Pareto and the Economics Club, IISER Bhopal

Pareto is a student-run club dedicated to exploring the best of economics, analytics, and decision-making. We host workshops, seminars, case competitions, and a flagship MUN committee every year. You can find our Instagram and LinkedIn pages here to know more about what we've been up to.
